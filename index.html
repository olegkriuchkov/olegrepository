A-Level: Front-End course

Semantic elements are elements with a meaning. A semantic element clearly describes its meaning ot both the browser and the developer. Examples of non-semantic elements: <div> and <span> - tells nothing about its content. Examples of semantic elements: <table> and <img> - clearly defines its content.
